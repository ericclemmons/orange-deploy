The minimal version needs to accomplish the following UX:

1. "Deploy to Cloudflare"
1. Connected to your account & your resources.
   Added benefit – your build steps could even run JS and access workers/resources
1. "Deploy your Application"
1. Connect to GitHub
   This will require a custom app
1. Pick a repo
1. Click "Deploy" button
1. Build starts for that commit
1. Cloudflare Sandbox starts up Container
1. Sandbox clones repo
1. Sandbox `npm install`
1. Sandbox `npm run build`
1. Sandbox `npx wrangler deploy`
